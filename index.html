Anna Zachariah — Lawyer in Toronto

I help people with refugee claims, immigration matters, family law, and other legal needs. My office is in Toronto. I work with clients across Ontario.

What I do

My practice covers:

  • Refugee law — claims before the Refugee Protection Division, appeals, and related matters
  • Immigration law — applications, sponsorships, and status questions
  • Family law — separation, custody, support, and child support orders
  • Estate law — wills and estate matters
  • CPP disability and benefits — applications and appeals
  • ODSP — applications and appeals
  • Consumer debt — debt-related legal help

Legal Aid Ontario

I take Legal Aid Ontario certificates. If you have one or think you might qualify, get in touch.

Many people who qualify for Legal Aid don't know a lawyer will accept their certificate. If you're not sure whether you qualify, Legal Aid Ontario can help you find out.
