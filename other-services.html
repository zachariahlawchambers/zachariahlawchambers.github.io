Other Services

In addition to refugee, immigration, and family law, I help with estate matters, government benefit appeals, and consumer debt.

Estate law

I help with wills and estate matters. If you want to put a will in place, or if you're dealing with the estate of someone who has died, I can help you understand what's involved and work through the necessary steps.

Estate matters can range from straightforward to complex, depending on the size of the estate and whether there are disputes. I'll give you a clear picture of what your situation involves before we proceed.

Canada Pension Plan (CPP) disability and benefits

If your application for CPP disability benefits has been refused, or if you've received a decision you think is wrong, you may be able to appeal. The appeal process has several stages, and having legal help can make a real difference.

I help with CPP disability applications and appeals before the Social Security Tribunal of Canada.

Ontario Disability Support Program (ODSP)

ODSP provides income and employment support to people with disabilities in Ontario. If your application has been refused or your benefits have been cut, you have the right to appeal.

I help with ODSP applications and appeals. The process can be long and the forms are complicated — I can help you put together a complete and accurate case.

Consumer debt

If you're dealing with debt — creditor calls, collection agencies, court actions — I can help you understand your options and what the law says about your situation.

There are legal protections for people dealing with debt. I'll explain what applies to your situation and what steps you can take.

Get in touch

If you have a question about any of these areas, send me a message. Tell me a bit about your situation and I'll get back to you as soon as I can.
