Immigration Law

Canada's immigration system is complex. I help people understand their options and work through the process.

What I help with

  • Permanent residence applications
  • Family sponsorships — spouses, children, parents, and other relatives
  • Temporary status — work permits, study permits, visitor extensions
  • Status questions — what your current status allows and what comes next
  • Restoration of status after it has lapsed
  • Humanitarian and compassionate applications
  • Citizenship applications and related matters

My experience

I have particular experience with immigration matters involving India — sponsorships, applications, and status questions for people with ties to India. I've also helped many clients with connections to Africa and other parts of the world.

Immigration applications involve forms, documents, timelines, and rules that change frequently. I'll help you put together a complete, accurate application and flag anything that needs extra attention.

Legal Aid Ontario

Legal Aid Ontario certificates are available for some immigration matters. If you have a certificate or think you might qualify, get in touch.

Check legalaid.on.ca to see whether your matter is covered.
